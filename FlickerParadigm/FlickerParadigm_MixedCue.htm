In this part of the test, you sometimes get a hint that will let you know whether to look out for a change in location of an object, a change in the color of an object, or whether an object was added/removed from the picture.


Otherwise the task is the same as before:

You will see a flickering presentation of pictures that contain one change.


TASK: Press the <%values.responsekey_label%> as soon as you notice the change; otherwise the flicker cycle continues for up to <%values.max_cycleduration/1000%> s


If you noticed the change a textbox will pop up and you are asked to describe the change in more detail; otherwise a new flicker cycle will begin.



Press the SPACEBAR to start the test.
